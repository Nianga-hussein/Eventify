Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the event?

You can easily register for the event through our website by visiting the registration page. Simply provide your details, select your ticket type, and complete the payment process.

What ticket options are available?

We offer various ticket types including Standard, VIP, and Group Passes. Each ticket type offers different perks, and you can select the one that suits your needs during registration.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your registration up to 7 days before the event for a full refund. Cancellations made after this period may incur a fee, depending on the ticket type.

Where can I find the event schedule?

The full event schedule is available on our website under the 'Agenda' section. You can view the detailed program, including keynote sessions, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Are there accommodation options available?

Yes, we have partnered with nearby hotels to offer special rates for our attendees. You can find the list of recommended accommodations and booking details in the 'Venue & Hotels' section of our website.
