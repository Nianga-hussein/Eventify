AI in Autonomous Vehicles

Discover the cutting-edge advancements in AI that are revolutionizing autonomous vehicles, from machine learning algorithms to sensor fusion and beyond.

Join industry leaders and AI experts as they explore the role of artificial intelligence in autonomous vehicle development. This session will cover the latest trends in autonomous driving, safety measures, AI-driven decision-making processes, and the future of intelligent transportation systems.

Session Agenda:

Below is the detailed agenda for the session:

  • Welcome and Opening Remarks
  • AI Algorithms in Self-Driving Cars
  • Sensor Fusion and Data Processing
  • Ethical and Legal Challenges in Autonomous Vehicles
  • The Future of AI in Transportation

Learning Outcomes:

  • Gain insights into AI-driven decision-making processes in autonomous vehicles.
  • Learn about the latest advancements in sensor technology and machine learning algorithms.
  • Understand the ethical and regulatory challenges surrounding autonomous vehicle deployment.
  • Explore the future of intelligent transportation systems and the role AI plays in this transformation.
